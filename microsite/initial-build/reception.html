Kirby and the RECEPTION

Critics hail it as one of the best Kirby games ever made.

Awards

Kirby and the Forgotten Land has received widely positive ratings from critics and players alike. Explore some of the praise Kirby received!

Reviews

According to Metacritic, Kirby and the Forgotten Land received "generally favorable reviews" with an aggregate score of 85, making it the second highest rated mainline Kirby game (and fourth overall Kirby game) on the site, behind Kirby Super Star (and Kirby: Canvas Curse and Kirby's Epic Yarn).

Destructoid rated the game 9.5/10.

GameSpot, GameRevolution, and Game Informer rated the game 9/10.

IGN and Electronic Gaming Monthly rated the game 8/10.

Critics primarily praised the creative stage design, more balanced difficulty, visuals, side content, and soundtrack, acknowledging the improvements made towards criticisms of the previous mainline game, Kirby Star Allies. Common criticisms of Kirby and the Forgotten Land include reduced multiplayer gameplay compared to Kirby Star Allies, and limited Copy Ability and enemy selections.

Sales

During the first week of sales, Kirby and the Forgotten Land sold 380,060 physical copies in Japan, making it the most successful launch of a Kirby game to date. In the United Kingdom, the game sold 2.5 times more physical copies than Kirby Star Allies at launch, making it the first Kirby game to reach #1 in sales, and the fifth most sold Kirby game in the country.

As of September 30, 2022, Kirby and the Forgotten Land has sold over 5.27 million units worldwide, making it the best-selling Kirby title to date.
