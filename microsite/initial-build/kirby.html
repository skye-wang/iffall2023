Kirby and the GAME

Kirby is sent through a dimensional vortex to a new world full of the crumbling ruins of a long-lost civilization, where he has to free Waddle Dees who have been trapped by a new villainous force called the Beast Pack. Accompanied by a flying mouse-like creature named Elfilin.

Mouthful Mode

Kirby enters this state when he inhales a sparkling object where he will stretch into the shape of said object. In this state, Kirby gains certain properties based on the object inhaled.
