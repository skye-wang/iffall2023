ENEMY

Imagine Dragons & JID

(from "Arcane League of Legends soundtrack")

produced by Mattman & Robin

ABOUT

“Enemy” is the lead single and fifth track for League of Legends‘ inaugural animated series, Arcane. The track features vocals from Imagine Dragons and JID, the former of which previously collaborated with Riot Games Music Team on “Warriors,” a song written and recorded as the anthem for the 2014 League of Legends World Championships.
